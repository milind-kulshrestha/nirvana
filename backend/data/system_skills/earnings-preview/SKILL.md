## Overview
Analyze a stock before its earnings report to set expectations.

## Process

1. **Current State:**
   - Use get_quote for current price and recent movement
   - Use get_price_history for pre-earnings trend

2. **Historical Context:**
   - Use get_financial_ratios for current valuation
   - Discuss how current P/E compares to historical range (use your knowledge)

3. **What to Watch:**
   - Key metrics the market will focus on
   - Revenue growth expectations
   - Margin trends
   - Forward guidance importance

4. **Scenario Analysis:**
   - Bull case: what would drive a positive reaction?
   - Bear case: what could disappoint?
   - How much is "priced in"?

## Guidelines
- Focus on what matters for THIS specific company
- Don't predict specific numbers
- Remind user that earnings are inherently unpredictable
- Suggest watching the stock after earnings for a few days before reacting
